Why We Still Make Furniture by Hand

It started with a love for natural texture. Bumi Kayu exists to bring the warmth of real solid teak into your home.

We see furniture as more than an object — it's something that stays in a family across generations. That's why we work only with select Perhutani teak, cared for by dedicated local artisans.

What We Believe

  • Simple & Elegant: We cut the unnecessary ornament and focus on pure function and clean, timeless lines.
  • Solid & Precise: We pair traditional woodworking with modern precision, so every piece holds up to daily life.
  • Sustainable & Responsible: We commit fully to certified, legally managed wood, so the forests we draw from stay in balance.

Why Choose Bumi Kayu?

  • 100% Genuine Solid Wood: We avoid engineered wood such as MDF or particleboard. Every piece shows the real character, grain, and color of premium teak.
  • The Work of Local Artisans: Every cut, assembly, and finish is carried out with care by skilled local craftspeople.
  • Comfort Made for Your Home: No two homes hold space the same way. We design with ergonomic proportions that look right and feel right, every day.

Our Vision

To be the go-to inspiration for Indonesian families creating a warm, comfortable, and elevated home through high-quality wood furniture.

Find the piece that will quietly witness your family's everyday moments. Enjoy exploring the Collection.
